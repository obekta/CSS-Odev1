ABOUT

Hi! I am Ozan Bektas. I decided to create this website in order to introduce you to my favourite pocket monsters from the famous (and my favourite) anime "Pokemon". Pokemon is an anime that is still going on. However, because I mostly watched it when I was a child, I primarily selected my favourite pokemons from the first seasons of the anime. Also, it can be said that dragon-type or snake-like pokemons are generally the ones that I liked most. I hope you will enjoy this website as much as I do!
